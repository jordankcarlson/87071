How to Save on iPhone

  1. Tap the Save Contact button above
  2. When the contact card appears, tap Create New Contact (or Add to Existing Contact)
  3. Tap Done in the top right corner

That's it! Future messages from 87071 will now show as "NCH MyChart Messages."

How to Save on Android

  1. Tap the Save Contact button above
  2. Your phone will download the contact file
  3. Open the downloaded file, or go to your Contacts app
  4. Tap Save or Import to add the contact

That's it! Future messages from 87071 will now show as "NCH MyChart Messages."

Visiting from a Computer?

This page works best on your mobile phone. To save this contact:

  1. Open this page on your iPhone or Android device
  2. Visit nchmd.org/87071 in your mobile browser
  3. Tap the Save Contact button to add it to your phone
